The name

Retzudo 烈 (れつ) 堂 (どう)

My nickname was inspired by the evil guy 柳生 烈堂 (Yagyū Retsudō) from Lone Wolf and Cub. I didn't watch the show much but I liked the name. I have no idea what it might translate to but Jisho.org tells me that 烈 is an adjective meaning "ardent, violent, vehement, furious, severe, extreme" while 堂 means "public chamber" or "hall". I have no idea what to make of that. Violent hall? Extreme chamber? Maybe a place where people are punished? No idea (tweet me @Retzudo if you have an idea).
